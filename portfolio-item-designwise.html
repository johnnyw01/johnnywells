Project Link

Project Description

DesignWise is an innovative architectural design start-up specializing in sustainable and breathtaking environments. They offer comprehensive services in architecture, interior design, and decoration, with a focus on creating spaces that are both functional and visually stunning. From seamlessly blending structures with their surroundings to personalized interior design solutions, DesignWise brings visions to life, transforming spaces into unique and harmonious designs.

Attention to detail is at the core of DesignWise's approach. They provide decoration services that enhance the beauty and functionality of spaces, ensuring meticulous integration of structures into designated areas through comprehensive architectural planning. With a commitment to overseeing the entire design process, from concept to execution, DesignWise guarantees that the final result perfectly aligns with their carefully crafted concepts. Their dedication to merging design with sustainability reflects their mission to contribute to a better world, pushing boundaries and leaving a lasting impression through the creation of breathtaking environments.

Project Details

  • The front-end of DesignWise was engineered with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, creating a responsive, interactive, and cross-device compatible website.
  • The site’s aesthetic appeal is amplified with the integration of diverse fonts from Google Fonts and icons from FontAwesome.
  • CSS enhances user engagement on the DesignWise website through a spinner animation and is used for layout, styling, interactivity, and responsiveness, creating a cohesive presentation, interactive elements, customized typography, and seamless multi-device experience.
  • The JavaScript code in DesignWise enhances usability by controlling site availability during loading, enabling smooth scrolling and mobile menu toggling. Incorporating jQuery, it adds interactive and dynamic features like tilt.js, contributing to an engaging and user-friendly experience on the website.
